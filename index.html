Features That Make Life Easier

Agenda is designed to be your daily companion for organizing your appointments in the best way possible

📅

Complete Appointment Management

Create, edit, and delete appointments with ease. Set priority, category, location, and notes. Full support for all-day and multi-day appointments.

🌙

Hijri & Gregorian Calendar

Display dates in both Hijri and Gregorian calendars simultaneously. Full Arabic language and RTL support included.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Precise appointment reminders with snooze and complete actions right from the notification. Automatic daily and weekly summaries. Up to 3 reminders per appointment.

🔁

Advanced Recurrence

Repeat appointments daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. Support for custom recurrence with specific days and optional end dates.

📊

Statistics & Analytics

Track your achievements with detailed statistics. View completed, cancelled, and upcoming appointments with clear charts.

📄

Ready-Made Templates

Save recurring appointments as templates for one-tap reuse. Save time with custom templates.

📎

Custom Categories

Organize appointments with color-coded custom categories. Built-in categories (Work, Personal, Health, Education) plus the ability to create your own.

📄

PDF Export

Export your appointments as professional PDF files. Daily, weekly, or monthly exports with sharing and direct copy options.

🔒

Security & Privacy

Protect your data with PIN or fingerprint. Private appointments are hidden in notifications. Full backup encryption.

📁

Backup & Restore

Full data backup with optional password encryption. Easy and secure restoration of all your appointments and settings.

📅

Multiple Calendar Views

Switch between daily, weekly, and monthly views. Color-coded dots indicate appointments on each day with a clear overview.

⚙

Tasker Integration

9 Tasker plugins for full automation: create appointments, query, complete, and more. Full control over your appointments through automation.
