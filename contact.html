Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes, Agenda is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.

Is my data safe?

Yes, all your data is stored locally on your device only. We don't collect or send any data to external servers. You can also protect the app with a PIN or fingerprint.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings then Backup. You can choose a regular backup or an encrypted one with a password. We recommend backing up regularly.

Does the app support the Hijri calendar?

Yes, the app displays dates in both Hijri and Gregorian calendars simultaneously.

How do I use Tasker plugins?

You need to install the Tasker app first. Then you'll find Agenda plugins in Tasker's plugin options (5 Actions, 2 Events, 2 States). Check our Tasker Guide for detailed instructions.

Notifications not working properly?

Make sure to:

  • Grant notification permission to the app
  • Disable battery optimization for the app
  • Allow the app to run in the background
  • Ensure Do Not Disturb mode is not enabled
